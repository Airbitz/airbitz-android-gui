Transaction List

Each transaction shows a grey value which is the running balance of your account at the time of that transaction.

Search your transactions by tapping on the magnifying glass icon in the upper right. Enter a Payee, Category, or text from a Notes field to search.

Tap the wallet name header at the top to list your other wallets, create a new wallet, rename a wallet, export your transactions, or to select another wallet for use.
