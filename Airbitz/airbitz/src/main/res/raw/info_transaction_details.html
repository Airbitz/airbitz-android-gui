Transaction Details

Tap in the top field (Payee or Business Name) to enter the name of the person or business that you transacted with. This field will autocomplete from businesses nearby and from your address book. You can always change this field at a later time.

Tap the Category button to choose a primary category for this transaction. Categories are either Income, Expense, Transfers, or Exchanges. Tap the category text field to choose a subcategory such as "Food and Dining". Type in a new category to add a category to the list.
